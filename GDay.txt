Predictability benefit od cloud computing

- The ablity to forecast and control the performance and behavior
  of a system.
- Includes the ability to predict future costs.

why is it needed?

- Predictability gives you the confidence that the system will
  continue to perform at the expected level in the future.
- And of course that you won't get a crazy bill unexpectedly.

How is it Achieved?

- Autoscaling
- Load balancing
- Different instance types, sizes, pricing tiers.
- cost management tools.
- API
- Pricing calculators
