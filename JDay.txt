Manageability

1. Management of the cloud
2. Management in the cloud

Management of the cloud
  - Templates
  - Automation
  - scaling
  - monitoring and alerts
  - self-healing

Management in the cloud
  - web portal
  - command line interface and scripts
  - APIs
  - PowerShell

Why it is needed?
  How easy it is to work with your applications in the cloud impacts
  cost, performance, security, and other priorites.

  Different cloud vendors are going to be easier or harder to work with.

How is it achieved?

  Azure portal, CLI, powershell, cloud shell, REST APIs, and other
  programmatic methods.

  Consolidated monitoring and alerting system.

  Ability to use ARM templates, Bicep, Terraform, etc.

  Autoscaling of most types of compute resources.
